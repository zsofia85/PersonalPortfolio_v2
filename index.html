the personal

A researcher turned UX designer, at the beginning of her creative journey. I am passionate about the research and visual processes of creating websites, slowly acquainting myself with the technical elements as well.

My aim is to acquire a well-rounded set of skills which will allow to help create simple, valuable and meaningful solutions.

When it is playtime, I am enjoying the outdoors while walking, running (Aalborg Atletik & Motion), swimming or organic farming. Highly appreciative of fireplaces, a cozy knitting project or a good book.
